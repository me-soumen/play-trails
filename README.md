## ðŸŽ® Game Details

### Sudoku
- **Grid Sizes**: 6x6 and 9x9
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Features**: Number validation, hint system, solve checker

### Picross
- **Grid Sizes**: 5x5 and 10x10
- **Puzzles**: 15 predefined 5x5 puzzles, 10 predefined 10x10 puzzles
- **Features**: Hint system, mistake tracking, clue-based solving

### Word Search
- **Grid Size**: 15x15
- **Words per Level**: 5 words
- **Word Pool**: 144+ words across 8 categories (Tech, Cities, Animals, Body Parts, Fruits, Vegetables, Countries, Subjects)
- **Features**: Level progression, new grids per level

### Memory
- **Difficulties**: Easy (8 pairs), Medium (15 pairs), Hard (18 pairs)
- **Features**: Moves tracking, timer, best score storage

### Color Match
- **Challenge**: Match colors from similar shades
- **Features**: Time-based gameplay, bonus time, difficulty scaling

### Math Quiz
- **Questions**: 10 questions per game
- **Operations**: Addition, subtraction, multiplication
- **Features**: Timer (30s per question), score tracking, best score

### Connect the Dots
- **Shapes**: 20+ different patterns (Star, Heart, Circle, Pentagon, Hexagon, Octagon, House, Arrow, Butterfly, Spiral, Zigzag, Cross, Flower, Swan, Boat, Airplane, Car, and more)
- **Features**: Mobile-optimized touch controls, picture reveal

### Tic Tac Toe
- **Players**: 2 players
- **Features**: Score tracking, auto-reset after win/draw

### Hangman
- **Categories**: 8 categories (Animals, Countries, Fruits, Sports, Objects, Colors, Actions, Nature)
- **Words**: 160+ words total (20 per category)
- **Features**: Visual hangman drawing, hints, keyboard support
