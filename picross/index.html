How to Play

Numbers show groups of consecutive filled cells in each row/column. Spaces between numbers indicate gaps.

Examples:
"5" = 5 cells in a row
"2 2" = 2 cells, gap, then 2 cells
"1 1 1" = 3 groups of 1 cell each
"3 2" = 3 cells, gap, then 2 cells

Click to fill, right-click (long-press) to mark empty.
